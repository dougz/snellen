About the Park

This is not a puzzle.

Founded in 1992, Penny Park has delighted countless visitors for almost three decades. Whether you're a thrill-seeking kid or simply young at heart, at Penny Park we guarantee you'll have a glorious day!

Judy Miller
Founder of Penny Park

A long-time lover of theme parks, Judy designed and built this magical place in 1992, naming it after her granddaughter Penny.

Penny Miller
Owner & Manager of Penny Park

For the last several years, Penny has run the show at the park. She is thrilled to be continuing the family tradition of bringing joy and wonder to people of all ages!

The Loonies

A Penny Park tradition, the Loonies are the lovable mascots of the park and can often be seen strolling around the grounds. If you see one, feel free to say hi!
