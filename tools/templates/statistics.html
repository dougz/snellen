Solve data

Here is a CSV file of all submissions during the hunt. The "result" column indicates what event this is recording:

  • open is when the puzzle was unlocked for this team. (The "input" and "canonical" fields will be empty.)
  • incorrect is when an incorrect answer was submitted.
  • correct means a correct answer for the puzzle was submitted.
  • requested means the submission triggered some reaction on the server (eg, a delivery or phone call from Guest Services)
  • solved means the puzzle was solved. Note that for Safari Adventure puzzles with multiple answers, the puzzle only becomes "solved" once all the answers have been submitted.
  • queued means the submission ran up against the anti-spamming limits and was delayed. Every queued submission should be followed by a later canceled record (if the team canceled the guess before the timer ran out), or a correct, incorrect, or requested record, if the submission was actually checked. The time of checking is what counts as the final submission time.
  • canceled means the submission was queued and canceled by the team before it was checked.
